About Us

The Student Development Council works in several areas related but not limited to career development, academics, social media outreach to school students, and alumni affairs - collectively termed as student development. We work closely with the Office of Alumni and International Relations (DAIR) as well as the Institute Career Development and Placement Council (ICDPC), in this regard.

Do contact us (Email: sdc@iiserb.ac.in) if you have any queries regarding our work. We are also constantly on the lookout for dedicated volunteers interested in contributing to our efforts in establishing a culture of career development at IISERB.
